Richymon

Web Designer&Game Developer

Career

Highly skilled web designer with extensive expertise in HTML, CSS, Git, Bootstrap, Sass, and continuous deployment using Vercel.
Specialist in optimizing functionality, efficient web architecture, and advanced SEO strategies to maximize digital visibility and performance.
We have experience working with GameMaker Language (GML) for game development, using its powerful tools to create well-structured mechanics, efficient systems, and engaging gameplay in 2D environments.
We also specialize in pixel art, allowing us to design characters, objects, interfaces, and environments with a distinctive, nostalgic, and stylized visual identity—perfect for indie and retro-style games.
In addition, we focus on crafting 2D environments and level design, paying close attention to both visual composition and functional layout, ensuring immersive, cohesive worlds that enhance the overall player experience.
Currently available to collaborate on challenging projects aiming for innovation and technical excellence.

Vision

We specialize in delivering high-quality projects with robust structure, flawless organization, and optimal functionality.
Our commitment is to ensure that our users have an exceptional experience by combining visually appealing design with an intuitive and efficient interface.
We strive to make every detail contribute to enjoyable navigation and outstanding performance.

Future Plans

We are currently actively learning JavaScript to enhance the functionality and interactivity of our web projects.
We are fully committed to continuous learning, always striving to grow alongside the ever-evolving world of technology.
Additionally, we are preparing to integrate backend functionalities, which will allow us to deliver a more complete and seamless user experience.
Our goals include implementing user authentication systems—so players can create their own accounts—and developing online-save game systems, providing a personalized and persistent gaming experience.
